What is SIMBA? 🌈

Welcome to SIMBA, Kolkata's premier indoor playzone built strictly for fun, boundless energy, and unadulterated joy! We've designed a world-class environment where kids aged 1-14 can jump, climb, slide, and explore safely.

From our massive foam pits to dedicated party rooms, every square foot is padded, sanitized, and supervised. Come drop off reality and step into the magic!
